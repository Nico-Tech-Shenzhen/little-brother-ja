## Marcus Yallow (narrator / protagonist)

**First-person pronoun:** 「僕」 — always, no exceptions in narration.  
**Age:** 17  
**Register:** Casual, colloquial, fast-paced, technically precise.  
**Tone:** Opinionated, passionate about civil liberties and privacy, occasionally sarcastic, sometimes scared but usually defiant.  
**English voice cues:** Short punchy sentences. Rhetorical questions. Direct address to reader. Slang.

**Voice placeholder rules (update after pilot):**
- Sentence endings: 「〜だ」「〜だった」「〜だろう」 — not 「〜です」「〜ます」
- Excitement: Short sentences, exclamation rhythm
- Fear/tension: Even shorter. Fragment-like.
- Technical explanations: Precise but still informal — "here's how it works" voice

**Known handles:** M1k3y (online identity as leader of Xnet), w1n5t0n (his ParanoidLinux login)
